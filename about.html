About

Hello! I'm Alexandria, an emerging artist based in North Carolina. My work is multifacited, combining my background in development with my life long love for the fine arts. I specialize in animations and illustrations, where I define my modern yet surreal aesthetic. I earned a dual Bachleor of Arts' in Studio Art and Computer Science from the Unversity of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. You can find some of my work published in the Cellar Door Literary Magazine, Carolina's oldest literary magazine.
