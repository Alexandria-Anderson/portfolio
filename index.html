For as long as I can remember, I have thought like an artist; art is successful if it sticks in your mind, if you can close your eyes and see the colors and shapes behind your eyelids. Successful design does the same; it stays with you. While earning my B.A. in Studio Art and B.A. in Computer Science has taught me that design should be accessible and intentional. It serves a purpose and yields influence, while still imbueing beauty into every corner of our lives. My dream as a professional creative is to work for a company where I can learn from like-minded peers. If this resonates with you, please reach out and let's make something cool together.
